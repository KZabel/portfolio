Kylee, in a nutshell...

Kylee Zabel is a government public relations specialist in Olympia, Washington. Her focus is on media relations and constituent outreach, as well as specific outreach relating to issues involving agriculture and natural resources in Washington state. She also is diversifying her portfolio by getting involved in web development and design for her team. A University of Washington grad, she loves calling Washington her home. Outside of work, Kylee calls herself 'a coffee fanatic and interior design enthusiast,' and loves cheering on her beloved Seattle Sounders Football Club.
